About Mediflow

Emali Limited, an incubatee from Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation (HKSTP) and a specialist in blockchain and cryptography technologies, will launch Mediflow, its latest application aimed at addressing claim management for the medical insurance sector.

Mediflow will expedite the application and approval process of insurance claims by securely and efficiently connecting insurance companies and medical practitioners. The service will save cost, eliminate cross-border duplicated claims, and provide reliable and relevant patient information to medical institutions and insurance companies. Mediflow will also allow patients to take full control of their own electronic health records, thanks to Emali's proprietary blockchain and cryptographic technologies that help ensure a high-level of data and privacy protection.
